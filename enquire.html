Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

For standard orders, we recommend 24-48 hours notice. For custom cakes and large orders, please allow 3-5 days.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have a selection of gluten-free and vegan baked goods. Please inquire about specific options.

Do you deliver?

We offer delivery within a 15km radius for orders over R250. Delivery fees apply based on distance.

Can I modify an existing order?

Changes to orders can be made up to 24 hours before the pickup/delivery time, subject to product availability.
